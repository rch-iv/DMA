# DMA Protocol Code of Conduct

Our community is built on mutual respect and a shared commitment to a more open, ethical, and human-centric digital future. We expect all participants to adhere to this Code of Conduct. It applies to all spaces and activities related to the DMA protocol.

### 1. Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

### 2. Unacceptable Behaviors
The following behaviors are considered harassment and are unacceptable:

- **Offensive Comments:** Offensive verbal or written comments, whether related to personal characteristics or general topics.
- **Intimidation:** Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following.
- **Disruption:** Sustained disruption of conversations, meetings, or public events.
- **Unwelcome Attention:** Unwelcome sexual or otherwise inappropriate attention or advances.
- **Trolling:** Any form of trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.

### 3. Reporting and Enforcement
If you witness or are subject to unacceptable behavior, or have any other concerns, please notify a member of the core team immediately.

Enforcement: All reports will be reviewed and investigated, leading to a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. The core team has the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments and contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
